Behind ITRFG — One Vision, One Founder

I'm Arif Shaik, the founder and director of ITRF (Global) Ltd.

ITRFG exists to protect and develop my intellectual-property models in a legally governed and partnership-driven manner. Rather than building large teams, I focus on creating solid ideas and working with the right organisations to bring them to life.

"A single mind can create many futures — but only with the right partners to execute them honestly and professionally."

Professional Background

An electronics engineering graduate who began his career as a mathematics teacher and then as an IT faculty member, I laid the foundation for what has become over 35 years of experience in information technology and entrepreneurship. I now serve as an active executive member in more than five global businesses — each aligned through a shared governance philosophy and value system

This cross-functional exposure has shaped how I design models that connect innovation with real-world delivery capability.

Value-Driven Innovation

Innovation is my field — and my nature. I build brands that add measurable value to every participant — partners, clients and end-users alike. My focus is never on volume but on purpose, impact and long-term credibility.

Personal Nature & Philosophy

I have always been driven by curiosity — a thirst to do more, learn faster and build better.

Never satisfied with what was achieved yesterday, I move on to the next idea the moment the current one finds its path. That restless nature has led me to connect and collaborate with hundreds of businesses worldwide — each joining under a common purpose to benefit one another and strengthen the industry itself.

My aim is not to compete, but to raise the standard — delivering outcomes that often surpass what larger organisations provide, ensuring clients and end users receive better value and faster innovation

Every initiative I develop is built on three principles:

•

Independence — ITRFG remains the IP owner and governance authority.

•

Integrity — Every partnership must be transparent and contractually sound.

•

Impact — Each idea must create tangible value for business and society.
