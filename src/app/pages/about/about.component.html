Behind ITRFG — One Vision, One Founder

Professional Background

With over 35 years of experience across information technology and entrepreneurship, I serve as an active executive member in more than five businesses globally — each aligned through a shared governance philosophy and value system.

This cross-functional exposure has shaped how I design models that connect innovation with real-world delivery capability.
